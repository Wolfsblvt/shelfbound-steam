# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want Shelfbound to be a welcoming, harassment-free space for everyone, regardless of background or
identity. We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, friendly, and respectful
community.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive. Assume good faith.
- Welcome newcomers and differing viewpoints; critique ideas, not people.
- Accept feedback gracefully and focus on what's best for the project and its users.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, insults, discriminatory or sexualized language, personal or political attacks,
publishing others' private information, or other conduct that would reasonably be considered
inappropriate.

## Enforcement

This project follows the spirit of the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/),
version 2.1. Report unacceptable behavior to the maintainer at **wolfsblvt@gmail.com**. Reports will
be handled discreetly. The maintainer may remove, edit, or reject contributions and comments, or ban
contributors, for behavior they deem inappropriate.
